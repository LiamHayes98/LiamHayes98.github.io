Hiro's Cave

Hiroâ€™s Cave is part of the fringing reef where there is a wall that drops from 14 meters to 22 meters. This exposes a multitude of ledges and caves. This dive site is better suited for experienced divers as it requires you to drift along the coral wall. On this dive you will encounter turtles, reef sharks, gropers, stingrays, eagle rays and schooling fish. You should also keep an eye out in the open water as manta rays and silvertip sharks tend to patrol the area.
