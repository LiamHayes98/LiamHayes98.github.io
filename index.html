After the first class, I am happy to say that I am even more excited about taking this class. In my discussions with other students, it seems that they have similar, as well as unique, interests that really helped me gauge what level I am at for my class and what I want to get out of this class. This class was a great introduction, and the other readings gave me a good overview of what kind of mindest I need to be in throughout this course.
