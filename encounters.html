Encounters

Encounters is the prime spot to scuba dive if you want to see most amount of unique marine life. This dive site is flooded with eagle and manta rays burying themselves beneath the sand. If you look closely you should spot reef sharks weaving their way through the coral reefs. More near the surface there is almost always around ten green turtles swimming around. It is by far the dive that is the best bang for your buck, as a you are guaranteed to see interesting marine life.
