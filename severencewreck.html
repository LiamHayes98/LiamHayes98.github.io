Severence Wreck

Severence Wreck is an amazing dive site that features a yacht that sank in 1998. It is resting 21 meters deep and is now covered head to toe in soft corals. There are many fish that circulate around the boat including sweetlips, snappers, lionfish, gropers, and thousands of shrimps. This location also has reef sharks, as well as an abundance of eagle and manta rays. This is a great place to scuba dive if you wish to see the diverse marine life of Lady Elliott Island.
