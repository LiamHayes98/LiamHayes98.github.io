Spider's Ledge

Spider's Ledge is by far the most fascinating dive site Lady Elliott Island has to offer. It is around 60 feet in depth and takes you along a 30 foot tall coral reef. It towers over you and has a large variance of vibrant coral. The entire reef looks like an underwater city or a scene straight out of Finding Nemo. This is the best diving spot if you are looking for a combination of diverse marine animals and coral.
