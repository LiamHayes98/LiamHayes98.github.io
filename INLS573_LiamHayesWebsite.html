Lady Elliott Island, the southernmost coral cay of the Great Barrier Reef in Australia, is a beautiful island where you can explore the diverse marine life within the flourishing coral reefs. Lady Elliot Island is located within the 'Green Zone' of the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park, which is the highest possible classification designated by the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Authority. Marine National Park Green Zones protect the biodiversity within the park by protecting important breeding and nursery areas such as seagrass beds, mangrove communities, deepwater shoals and reefs.
