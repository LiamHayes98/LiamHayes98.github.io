Anemone Bommie

Anemone Bommie lives up to it's name by having a ridiculous amount of sea anemones. It actually contains around 40 percent of all sea anemones around the island. They serve as a home to clown fish and are surrounded by a habitat full of fish and coral reefs. This area is only 30 feet deep and is great for beginner divers. It is also a great place to see green turtles and trumpet fish.
